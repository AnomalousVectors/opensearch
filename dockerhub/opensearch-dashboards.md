# Anomalous Vectors OpenSearch Dashboards

Custom OpenSearch Dashboards image to support agentic pentest research. Explore and analyze structured data from the companion OpenSearch stack in the UI for manual, automated, or agentic workflows.

Pair with [`anomalousvectors/opensearch`](https://hub.docker.com/r/anomalousvectors/opensearch).
